<p><ul><li>Bone Growth Stimulators (BGS)</li><li>Viscosupplements</li><li>Bone Graft Substitute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Orthobiologics market encompasses various types, including Bone Growth Stimulators (BGS), Viscosupplements, and Bone Graft Substitutes. BGS devices use electrical or electromagnetic fields to enhance bone healing and regeneration. Viscosupplements are injectable treatments that help relieve joint pain by restoring synovial fluid in conditions like osteoarthritis. Bone Graft Substitutes offer alternative materials that support bone growth, healing fractures, and enhancing orthopedic surgeries without the need for autologous grafts. Together, these products aid in musculoskeletal recovery and repair.</p></p>
