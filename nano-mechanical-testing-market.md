<p><p>The nano-mechanical testing market encompasses various techniques for assessing material properties at the nanoscale. Nano indentation involves measuring hardness and elastic modulus by pressing a probe into a material. Nano scratch testing evaluates scratch resistance and adhesion. Nano-impact testing assesses material toughness and resistance to impact forces, while nano-tribological testing examines friction and wear behavior at small scales. Other methods may include nano tensile testing and nano-compression testing, broadening the evaluation of material characteristics and performance in diverse applications.</p></p>
