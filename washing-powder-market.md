<p><strong>The Washing Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-phosphorus</li><li>Phosphorus</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The washing powder market can be classified into two main types: non-phosphorus and phosphorus-based products. Non-phosphorus washing powders are formulated without phosphates, making them more environmentally friendly and suitable for regions with strict regulations on water pollution. On the other hand, phosphorus-based washing powders contain phosphates, which enhance cleaning efficiency by softening water and removing stains. However, their environmental impact, particularly in terms of nutrient pollution in water bodies, has led to a growing preference for non-phosphorus alternatives.</p></p>
