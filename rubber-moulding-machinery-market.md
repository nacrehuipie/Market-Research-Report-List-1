<p><p>The rubber moulding machinery market can be divided into two primary types: semi-automatic and automatic rubber moulding machinery. Semi-automatic machinery requires both manual and automated processes, allowing operators to oversee certain tasks while benefiting from some mechanization. In contrast, automatic rubber moulding machinery operates with minimal human intervention, maximizing efficiency and consistency in production. Both types cater to different operational needs, with semi-automatic systems often favored by smaller businesses for flexibility, while automatic systems are preferred for high-volume production.</p></p>
