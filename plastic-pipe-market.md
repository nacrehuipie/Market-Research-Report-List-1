<p><p>The plastic pipe market includes various types, each suited for specific applications. PVC pipes are popular for their durability and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for water supply and drainage. HDPE pipes are known for their high tensile strength and flexibility, used in gas and water distribution. Fiberglass pipes offer lightweight strength and corrosion resistance, often utilized in industrial applications. ABS pipes are cost-effective and used mainly in drainage systems. CPVC pipes can withstand higher temperatures, making them suitable for hot water systems.</p></p>
