<p><p>The organic carrot flour market primarily includes two key types: spray-drying carrot powder and freeze-dried carrot powder. Spray-drying involves rapidly evaporating moisture from pureed carrots, resulting in a fine powder that retains flavor and nutrients, making it ideal for various culinary applications. In contrast, freeze-dried carrot powder is produced through a process that preserves the carrots' structure and nutritional content even further, providing a more concentrated taste and vibrant color. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers and are used in food products, snacks, and supplements.</p></p>
