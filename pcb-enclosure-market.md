<p><strong>The PCB Enclosure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Shell</li><li>Non-metallic Shell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB enclosure market is divided into two primary types: metal shell and non-metallic shell enclosures. Metal shell enclosures offer robust durability, excellent shielding against electromagnetic interference (EMI), and high heat dissipation, making them suitable for industrial and high-frequency applications. Conversely, non-metallic shell enclosures, often made from materials like plastic or composite, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and cost-effective, making them ideal for consumer electronics and less demanding environments. Each type serves specific needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
