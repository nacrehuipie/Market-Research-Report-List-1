<p><p>The printing inks market encompasses various types, each suited for specific printing methods. Lithographic inks are oil-based, ideal for offset printing on paper and cardboard, offering high-quality images. Flexographic inks are quick-drying and water-based, suitable for labels and packaging. Digital inks cater to inkjet and laser printing processes, providing versatility and customization. Gravure inks, used in rotary printing, deliver vibrant colors and high-volume production, making them perfect for magazines and promotional materials. Each type enhances specific applications effectively.</p></p>
