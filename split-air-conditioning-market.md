<p><ul><li>Mono-split</li><li>Multi-split</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The split air conditioning market is divided into two primary types: mono-split and multi-split systems. Mono-split systems consist of one indoor unit connected to a single outdoor condenser, making them ideal for individual rooms or small spaces. In contrast, multi-split systems feature multiple indoor units connected to a single outdoor unit, allowing for temperature control across several rooms simultaneously. This flexibility caters to diverse cooling needs, making both options popular in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
