<p><p>The Near-infrared Spectroscope market comprises three main types: FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near-Infrared), UV-Vis-NIR (Ultraviolet-Visible Near-Infrared), and Other technologies. FT-NIR offers high spectral resolution and rapid analysis, making it ideal for complex samples in various industries, such as pharmaceuticals and food. UV-Vis-NIR combines UV and visible light spectroscopy, providing broader wavelength coverage for diverse applications. Other market types may include handheld and portable devices, catering to specific needs like field analysis and environmental monitoring, enhancing accessibility and flexibility.</p></p>
