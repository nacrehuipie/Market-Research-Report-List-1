<p><p>The float valves market primarily encompasses two types: angle float valves and round float valves. Angle float valves are designed with an L-shaped configuration, enabling efficient flow regulation in space-constrained applications, often used in tanks and sump systems. Round float valves, characterized by their circular shape, provide reliable performance in larger systems, helping maintain water levels in various settings, from irrigation to industrial processes. Both types ensure optimal fluid management by activating or deactivating based on float position.</p></p>
