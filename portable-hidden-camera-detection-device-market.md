<p><p>The Portable Hidden Camera Detection Device market consists of two primary types: RF-based and LED light gauge-based devices. RF-based detectors identify hidden cameras by scanning for radio frequency signals emitted from wireless devices, making them effective for detecting both conventional and modern hidden cameras. In contrast, LED light gauge-based devices utilize a specialized light source to reveal camera lenses, even in low light conditions. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing privacy and security by unveiling unauthorized surveillance.</p></p>
