<p><p>PLGA market types are classified based on the molar ratios of lactic acid to glycolic acid, influencing their degradation rates and applications. PLGA 50:50 offers a balanced degradation, suitable for short-term drug delivery. PLGA 65:35 and 75:25 provide slower degradation, ideal for sustained-release formulations. PLGA 85:15 has the slowest degradation, often used for long-term applications. Other variations exist to cater to specific therapeutic needs and enhance drug stability, demonstrating versatility in biomedical applications.</p></p>
