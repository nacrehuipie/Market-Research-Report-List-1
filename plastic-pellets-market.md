<p><p>The plastic pellets market consists of various types based on the polymer composition, each serving different applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and used for bags. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is strong, ideal for bottles. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is durable for containers. PE (Polyethylene) is versatile, while PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is used in pipes. PP (Polypropylene) is resistant to heat, making it suitable for automotive parts. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) offers toughness, commonly found in consumer products. Other types include specialty polymers with niche applications.</p></p>
