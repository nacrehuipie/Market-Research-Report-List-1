<p><p>The artificial knee joint components market comprises several key types, each essential for joint functionality. The femoral end implant acts as the thigh bone's replacement, allowing for smooth articulation. The tibia articular surface liner provides cushioning between the femur and tibia, enhancing movement. The tibia base anchors the joint to the lower leg, ensuring stability. The patella implant replaces the kneecap, facilitating knee extension and flexion. Other market components may include surgical instruments and fixation devices to support implantation and alignment.</p></p>
