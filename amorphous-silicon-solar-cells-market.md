<p><p>Amorphous silicon solar cells are categorized into two main market types: single junction and multi-junction. Single junction cells consist of a single layer of amorphous silicon, offering a simpler structure and lower manufacturing costs, but with lower efficiency compared to multi-junction cells. Multi-junction solar cells combine multiple layers of different semiconductor materials, enhancing light absorption and overall efficiency. This technology allows for better performance in varying lighting conditions, making it ideal for specialized applications and high-performance needs in the solar market.</p></p>
