<p><ul><li>Static Pressure Mattress</li><li>Alternating Pressure Mattress</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure relief mattress market is primarily divided into two types: static pressure mattresses and alternating pressure mattresses. Static pressure mattresses distribute weight evenly across their surface, reducing pressure points and enhancing comfort for individuals at risk of bedsores. In contrast, alternating pressure mattresses use a dynamic air system to continually change pressure across different areas, promoting blood circulation and further preventing skin breakdown. Both types are essential in clinical and home care settings for enhancing patient comfort and safety.</p></p>
