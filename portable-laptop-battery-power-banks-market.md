<p><p>The portable laptop battery power bank market features three primary battery types: Nickel Cadmium (NiCad), Nickel Metal Hydride (NiMH), and Lithium Ion (Li-Ion). NiCad batteries are known for their durability and reliability but suffer from memory effect and lower energy density. NiMH batteries offer higher capacity compared to NiCad and are more environmentally friendly. Lithium Ion batteries dominate the market due to their lightweight, high energy density, and rapid charging capabilities, making them the preferred choice for modern laptops.</p></p>
