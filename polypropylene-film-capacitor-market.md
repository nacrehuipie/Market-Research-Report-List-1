<p><ul><li>Plain Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li><li>Metallised Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene film capacitor market is primarily divided into two types: plain polypropylene film capacitors and metallised polypropylene film capacitors. Plain polypropylene film capacitors consist of thin polypropylene sheets and are known for their stable performance and lower cost. In contrast, metallised polypropylene film capacitors feature a layer of metal deposited on the film, enhancing capacitance and efficiency while providing self-healing capabilities. Both types are widely used in electronic circuits, energy storage, and filtering applications, each offering distinct advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
