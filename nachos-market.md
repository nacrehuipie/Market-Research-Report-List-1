<p><p>Nachos can be categorized into two main market types: Fried and Baked. Fried nachos are typically made from corn tortillas that are deep-fried until crispy, offering a rich, indulgent flavor and texture. On the other hand, baked nachos involve tortillas that are baked until crunchy, providing a lighter and often healthier alternative. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with fried nachos appealing to those seeking traditional, hearty snacks, while baked nachos attract health-conscious individuals looking for guilt-free indulgence.</p></p>
