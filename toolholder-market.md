<p><p>The toolholder market encompasses various types, each designed for specific machining applications. Hydraulic expansion toolholders utilize hydraulic pressure for secure tool clamping, offering precision and vibration dampening. Heat shrinking toolholders use thermal methods to expand and contract for tool insertion and removal, ensuring high accuracy. Milling chucks provide a robust grip for milling operations, while collet chucks enable versatile tool change. Drill chucks are specialized for drilling applications, offering quick tool swaps. Other toolholder types include specialized designs for specific machining needs.</p></p>
