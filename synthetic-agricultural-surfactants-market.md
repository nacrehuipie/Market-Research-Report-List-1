<p><p>The Synthetic Agricultural Surfactants Market comprises four main types: non-ionic, anionic, cationic, and amphoteric. Non-ionic surfactants are neutral and enhance wetting and spreading, making them ideal for herbicides and pesticides. Anionic surfactants carry a negative charge, improving emulsification and stability in formulations. Cationic surfactants have a positive charge and are effective in soil conditioning and promoting adherence of products. Amphoteric surfactants can behave as either cationic or anionic based on pH, providing versatility in agricultural applications.</p></p>
