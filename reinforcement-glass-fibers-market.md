<p><p>The Reinforcement Glass Fibers market is categorized into Polymer Composites and Non-Polymer Composites. Non-Polymer Composites, on the other hand, incorporate glass fibers in matrices other than polymers, such as metal or ceramic-based materials, offering unique properties suitable for specialized applications. Both types provide improved structural integrity, lightweight characteristics, and resistance to environmental factors.</p></p>
