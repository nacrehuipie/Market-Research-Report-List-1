<p><p>The cardiac ablation market comprises various techniques designed to treat arrhythmias. Electrical cardiac ablation utilizes heat generated by radiofrequency energy to destroy abnormal heart tissue. Cryoablation employs extreme cold to achieve a similar effect, often with fewer complications. Ultrasound cardiac ablation utilizes high-frequency sound waves to target and eliminate problematic areas within the heart. Each method offers distinct advantages regarding precision, recovery time, and patient outcomes, catering to diverse clinical needs and preferences in the management of cardiac conditions.</p></p>
