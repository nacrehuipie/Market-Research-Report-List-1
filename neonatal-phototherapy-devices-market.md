<p><ul><li>Phototherapy Lamp</li><li>Phototherapy Blanket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neonatal phototherapy devices market comprises two main types: phototherapy lamps and phototherapy blankets. Phototherapy lamps emit specific wavelengths of light to effectively treat jaundice in newborns by breaking down excess bilirubin in the blood. These devices are typically positioned above the infant. In contrast, phototherapy blankets, also known as fiberoptic blankets, are portable and wrap around the baby, delivering light therapy while allowing for more mobility. Both types are essential in neonatal care to promote healthy bilirubin levels.</p></p>
