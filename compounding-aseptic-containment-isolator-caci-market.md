<p><p>Compounding Aseptic Containment Isolators (CACIs) are specialized equipment used in pharmaceutical and healthcare settings to ensure a sterile environment for compounding drugs. The market is divided into two main types: recirculating and total exhaust systems. Recirculating CACIs filter and recirculate air within the isolator, maintaining sterility while preserving energy efficiency. In contrast, total exhaust CACIs expel all air to the outside, ensuring a higher level of containment and safety, making them suitable for handling highly hazardous materials.</p></p>
