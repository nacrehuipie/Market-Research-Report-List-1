<p><ul><li>General Heat Treatment</li><li>Surface Heat Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat treating service market comprises two main types: general heat treatment and surface heat treatment. General heat treatment involves processes like annealing, quenching, and tempering, aimed at altering the overall properties of materials for enhanced performance and durability. In contrast, surface heat treatment focuses on modifying the exterior layer of materials to improve hardness, wear resistance, and fatigue strength while maintaining a softer core. Both types serve various industries, ensuring materials meet specific requirements for optimal functionality.</p></p>
