<p><strong>The Immunochromatographic Assay Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sandwich Assay</li><li>Competitive Assay</li><li>Multiplex Detection Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochromatographic assay kit market consists of three primary types: Sandwich Assay, Competitive Assay, and Multiplex Detection Assay. The Sandwich Assay involves the binding of two antibodies to target analytes, enhancing specificity and sensitivity. Competitive Assay, on the other hand, measures the concentration of an analyte based on the competition between the sample and a conjugated antigen, offering quick results. Multiplex Detection Assay enables the simultaneous detection of multiple targets in a single test, increasing efficiency and throughput in diagnostics.</p></p>
