<p><p>The surgical fixators market is divided into internal and external fixators. Internal fixators include plates, screws, and nails that stabilize fractures from within the body, promoting healing without external support. External fixators, on the other hand, are applied outside the body and include unilateral and bilateral fixators, circular fixators, and hybrid fixators, which provide adjustable stabilization and are often used in complex fractures or deformities. Together, these fixators enhance surgical outcomes and patient recovery.</p></p>
