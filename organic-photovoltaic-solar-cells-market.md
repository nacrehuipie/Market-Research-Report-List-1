<p><p>The Organic Photovoltaic Solar Cells market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Layer and Bilayer Organic Photovoltaic Solar Cells. Single Layer cells consist of a single organic layer that absorbs sunlight and generates electricity, offering a simpler manufacturing process but typically lower efficiency. In contrast, Bilayer cells comprise two distinct organic layers, enhancing light absorption and improving energy conversion efficiency. Both types are lightweight, flexible, and have potential applications in various settings, contributing to the growing renewable energy sector.</p></p>
