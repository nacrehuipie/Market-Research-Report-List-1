<p><p>The transformer accessories market can be categorized into low, medium, and high voltage transformers, each serving distinct applications. Low voltage transformers typically handle voltages up to 1 kV, primarily found in residential and commercial setups. Medium voltage transformers operate between 1 kV and 36 kV, often used in industrial and utility applications. High voltage transformers exceed 36 kV, critical for power transmission over long distances. Each category requires specific accessories, such as cooling systems, protective devices, and monitoring tools, to ensure efficient performance and reliability.</p></p>
