<p><ul><li>Single Volute Pump</li><li>Double Volute Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The axially split single-stage pump market includes two primary types: single volute and double volute pumps. Single volute pumps feature one spiral casing that directs fluid towards the impeller, ideal for applications requiring simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, double volute pumps have two volutes, reducing radial forces on the impeller and enhancing efficiency, particularly in high-flow scenarios. Both types serve various industries, including water treatment, chemical processing, and HVAC, offering distinct advantages based on operational demands and fluid handling requirements.</p></p>
