<p><p>The fecal pancreatic elastase testing market includes two primary types: Fecal Pancreatic Elastase ELISA Kits and Rapid Fecal Pancreatic Elastase Kits. ELISA Kits utilize enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay technology for accurate quantification of elastase levels in stool samples, aiding in diagnosing pancreatic exocrine insufficiency.
