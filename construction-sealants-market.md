<p><strong>The Construction Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moisture Cured</li><li>Heat Cured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction sealants market is categorized primarily into moisture-cured and heat-cured types. Moisture-cured sealants rely on atmospheric moisture to initiate the curing process, making them ideal for outdoor applications where humidity is prevalent. They offer excellent adhesion and durability. In contrast, heat-cured sealants require elevated temperatures to cure, providing strong bonds in industrial settings. Both types play crucial roles in construction, ensuring structural integrity, weatherproofing, and enhancing the longevity of building materials.</p></p>
