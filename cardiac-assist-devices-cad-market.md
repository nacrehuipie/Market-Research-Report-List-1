<p><p>Cardiac Assist Devices (CAD) are medical devices designed to support heart function in patients with severe heart conditions. The market includes several types:</p><p>1. **Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs)**: These are used to support one or both ventricles, improving blood flow and reducing the heart's workload.</p><p>2. **Total Artificial Heart**: This device replaces the heart entirely, designed for patients awaiting transplants or those not eligible.</p><p>3. **Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump (IABP)**: This device enhances myocardial oxygen supply and reduces cardiac workload by improving coronary blood flow during the diastole phase of the cardiac cycle.</p></p>
