<p><p>The three-phase dry type transformer market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Double Winding Transformer**: This design features two sets of windings, allowing efficient voltage transformation between high and low voltages, commonly used in industrial applications.</p><p>2. **Three Winding Transformer**: Equipped with three sets of windings, this transformer can manage multiple voltage levels, making it ideal for complex power distribution systems.</p><p>3. **Self-Coupled Transformer**: Utilizing a single winding for both primary and secondary circuits, this transformer provides effective voltage regulation and is often used in specialized applications requiring compact design.</p></p>
