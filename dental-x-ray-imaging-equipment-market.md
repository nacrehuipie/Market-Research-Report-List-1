<p><strong>The Dental X-Ray Imaging Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-ray Imaging</li><li>Extraoral X-ray Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray imaging equipment market is categorized into two main types: intraoral and extraoral X-ray imaging. Intraoral X-ray imaging involves capturing images from inside the mouth, providing detailed views of individual teeth and surrounding structures, often used for diagnosis and treatment planning. Extraoral X-ray imaging, on the other hand, captures images from outside the mouth, covering larger areas and including panoramic and cephalometric imaging, making it suitable for evaluating jaw relationships and overall dental and facial structure.</p></p>
