<p><strong>The CMOS Image Sensor for Vehicle-Mounted Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated (FSI) CMOS Sensor</li><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS image sensors play a crucial role in vehicle-mounted cameras, enhancing safety and driver assistance systems. The front-illuminated (FSI) CMOS sensor captures light through a front layer, making it cost-effective but often less sensitive in low-light conditions. In contrast, the backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensor features a design that allows light to hit the photodiodes directly, resulting in improved sensitivity and performance in low-light situations. Together, these sensor types cater to varying market needs from cost efficiency to enhanced imaging capabilities.</p></p>
