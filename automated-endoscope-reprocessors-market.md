<p><p>The Automated Endoscope Reprocessors (AER) market comprises two main types: single-chamber and multi-chamber systems. Single-chamber AERs are compact, with one chamber that cleans and disinfects endoscopes in a single process, making them suitable for smaller facilities with limited space. In contrast, multi-chamber AERs feature separate chambers for washing, rinsing, and disinfection, allowing for higher throughput and greater efficiency in larger healthcare settings, where multiple endoscopes may need reprocessing simultaneously to meet patient demand.</p></p>
