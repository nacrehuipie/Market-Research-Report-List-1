<p><strong>The Water-Jet Propulsion Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Axial Flow Waterjet"</li><li>"Mixed Flow Waterjet"</li><li>"Centrifugal Flow Waterjet"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-jet propulsion device market includes three primary types: axial flow waterjets, mixed flow waterjets, and centrifugal flow waterjets. Axial flow waterjets propel water parallel to the jet's axis, providing efficient thrust for small vessels. Mixed flow waterjets combine features of axial and centrifugal designs, offering versatility suitable for a range of applications. Centrifugal flow waterjets use a rotating impeller to accelerate water outward, generating high pressure and thrust, ideal for larger marine vessels. Each type addresses specific operational requirements and vessel designs.</p></p>
