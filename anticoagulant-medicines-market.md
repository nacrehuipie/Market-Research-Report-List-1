<p><strong>The Anticoagulant Medicines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injectable Anticoagulant Medicines</li><li>Oral Anticoagulant Medicines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anticoagulant medicines market is divided into two main types: injectable and oral anticoagulants. Injectable anticoagulants, such as heparins, are administered via injection and are often used in hospital settings for rapid anticoagulation, especially in emergency cases. Oral anticoagulants, like warfarin and direct oral anticoagulants (DOACs), are taken as pills and are suitable for long-term management of conditions like atrial fibrillation or venous thromboembolism. Both types play crucial roles in preventing blood clots and managing various cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
