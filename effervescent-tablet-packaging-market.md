<p><p>Effervescent tablet packaging primarily includes tube packaging and foil wrapping. Tube packaging is designed for convenience and protection, offering airtight seals that preserve the tablets' potency and prevent moisture exposure. This format often allows for easy dispensing and portability. Foil wrapping, on the other hand, provides individual protective layers for each tablet, ensuring freshness and safeguarding against environmental factors. Both packaging types cater to consumer preferences for safety, ease of use, and effective preservation of effervescent formulations.</p></p>
