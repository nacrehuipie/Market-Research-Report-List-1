<p><strong>The Antifouling Paint for Boats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Polishing Copolymer (SPC) Coatings</li><li>Fouling Release Coatings (FRC)</li><li>Ablative Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antifouling paint for boats is crucial for maintaining performance and longevity. Self-Polishing Copolymer (SPC) coatings gradually release biocides as they erode, ensuring consistent protection. Fouling Release Coatings (FRC) create a smooth surface that allows barnacles and algae to be easily removed. Ablative types wear away over time, releasing active ingredients to prevent fouling while benefiting from gradual renewal. Other types may include hard paints with varying biocide formulations, offering tailored solutions for different marine environments and vessel needs.</p></p>
