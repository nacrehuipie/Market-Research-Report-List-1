Backup rings enhance the performance of O-rings, especially in dynamic environments, by preventing extrusion. Other seals might include gaskets and custom shapes, catering to specific industrial needs. Together, these components ensure effective sealing in diverse applications, offering durability and resistance to chemicals.</p></p>
