<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic cartoning machine market includes two main types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS) machines. V-FFS machines create bags from flat film, filling and sealing them in a vertical orientation, ideal for products like snacks and powders. In contrast, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, filling and sealing cartons primarily for bulkier items like bottles and boxes. Both systems enhance packaging efficiency and are essential for various industries, balancing automation and manual processes.</p></p>
